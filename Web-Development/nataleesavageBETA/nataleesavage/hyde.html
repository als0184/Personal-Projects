About
"Band Geeks" is a spirited and captivating musical created by Tommy Newman. Set in the world of high school marching bands, this musical takes audiences on a lively journey filled with humor, heart, and the power of music. The story revolves around a group of misfit band members who come together to prove that they are more than just geeks. Led by an ambitious and determined protagonist, the band faces challenges, rivalries, and personal growth as they strive for success in a high-stakes band competition. With a vibrant score and catchy tunes, "Band Geeks" celebrates the unifying force of music, the strength of friendship, and the triumph of embracing one's true self. It's a joyous and uplifting musical experience that resonates with audiences of all ages, reminding us that sometimes the biggest victories come from unexpected places.
